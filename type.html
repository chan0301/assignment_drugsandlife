Cannabisc

Also known as:

Bongo/ Ganja/ Grass/ Marijuana/ Pot/ Thai sticks

Description:

These are tobacco-like greenish or brownish material made up of the dried flowering tops and leaves of the cannabis (hemp) plant.

Effects:

Cannabis can make users feel relaxed and heighten their sensory awareness. Thus, users may experience a more vivid sense of sight, smell, taste and hearing.

Cocaine

Also known as: Crack /Bazooka/ Blanche/ Cake/ Coke/ Lady/ Star dust

Description:

Cocaine is a fine white or off-white powder that acts as a powerful stimulant. In its pure form, cocaine is extracted from the leaves of the coca plant.

Effects:

Cocaine can make users feel exhilarated and euphoric. Furthermore, users often experience a temporary increase in alertness and energy levels, and a postponement of hunger and fatigue.

Ecstasy

Also known as: E/ Snackies/ New Yorkers/ Disco biscuits

Description:

Ecstasy is a psychoactive stimulant. It is usually distributed as a tablet or pill but can also be a powder or capsule. The tablets can be in many different shapes and sizes.

Effects:

Ecstasy can heighten users' empathy levels and induce a feeling of closeness to people around them. It is often used at "rave parties" to increase participants' sociability and energy levels.

Hallucinogens

Also known as: LSD/ D-Lysergic/ Acid/ Diethylamide

Description:

Hallucinogens, or "psychedelics", are drugs that alter users' state of consciousness and produce different kinds of hallucinations.

Effects:

Taking LSD leads to strong changes in thought, mood and senses in addition to feelings of empathy and sociability. However, the exact effects of LSD vary depending on the mental state of the user and the environment when taking the drug.

Heroin

Also known as: Smack/ Brown/ H/ Horse/ Junk/ Harry/ White lady

Description:

Heroin is an addictive drug with painkilling properties processed from morphine, a naturally occurring substance from the Asian opium poppy plant.

Effects:

Heroin can make users feel an initial surge of euphoria, along with a feeling of warmth and relaxation. Users also often become detached from emotional or physical distress, pain or anxiety.

Methamphetamine

Also known as: Crack meth/ Ice/ Crystal meth/ Tik/ Shabu/ Yaba

Description:

Methamphetamine is part of the group of drugs called amphetamine-type stimulants (ATS). It is a synthetic drug that is usually manufactured in illegal laboratories.

Effects:

Methamphetamine stimulates a feeling of physical and mental well being, as well as a surge of euphoria and exhilaration.
